Chicken & Rice

  • Time: 1.5 Hours
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1 lb
Rice 2 cups
Chicken Broth 16 oz
Water 1 cup
Butter/Margarine 2 tbsp
Black Pepper 2 tsp
Rosemary 1 tsp
Garlic 1 clove

Step 1: Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.

Step 2: Mix Rice, Chicken Broth, Spices, and Water in a baking pan.

Step 3: Mix in Butter.

Step 4: Cover pan with aluminum foil.

Step 5: Place pan in oven.

Step 6: Cook for 1.25 hours.

Step 7: Remove from oven and let cool.

Peanut Butter & Jam

  • Time: .2 Hours
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Ezekiel Bread 2 slices
Peanut Butter 2 tbsp
Jam (your choice) 1 tbsp

Step 1: Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.

Step 2: Spread jam on the other slice of bread.

Step 3: Press both pieces together with the substance side facing each other.

Step 4: Devour said sandwich.
